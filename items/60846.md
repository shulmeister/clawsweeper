## Summary

Keep #60846 open. Current main still only evaluates `failureAlert` for `result.status === "error"`; skipped runs reset `consecutiveErrors` and `lastFailureAlertAtMs`, and there is no skip counter, `skipAlert`, or `includeSkipped` contract in state/schema/docs. The related PR #60876 attempted this exact fix but is closed unmerged, and the later open #62777 does not clearly supersede the skipped-run monitoring request.

## Evidence

The `else` branch covers `ok` and `skipped`, resetting `consecutiveErrors` and `lastFailureAlertAtMs` instead of evaluating an alert threshold.
