## Summary

Keep open. The current `main` branch still routes `channels.discord.voice.daveEncryption` straight into `@discordjs/voice` and fails the join on any pre-`Ready` abort, with no non-DAVE-specific fallback or fix visible in code, docs, tests, or changelog. The issue is also recent and the discussion narrowed it to a distinct `/vc join` handshake failure, separate from both the older Carbon forwarding gap and the later TTS playback problem.

## Evidence

- **Current join path still fails hard on pre-Ready aborts:** `DiscordVoiceManager.join()` reads `discordConfig.voice?.daveEncryption`, passes it directly to `joinVoiceChannel()`, then waits for `VoiceConnectionStatus.Ready` for 15s and returns `Failed to join voice channel: ...` on any error. I do not see a non-DAVE workaround, retry, or alternate handshake path here.
