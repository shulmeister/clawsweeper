## Summary

Current main still has the reported Slack identity gap. `dispatchPreparedSlackMessage` resolves the agent identity and normal/fallback delivery can pass it to `sendMessageSlack`, but the draft preview stream still cannot receive or forward identity to its initial `chat.postMessage`, and bare Slack emoji aliases are still forwarded raw on the monitor path. Related PRs #37836/#37839 are closed unmerged, while #38235/#38237 remain the active follow-up path rather than evidence that this is fixed.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the bug open until the Slack plugin implementation is fixed or maintainers explicitly consolidate it into #38235/#38237. The likely fix belongs in `extensions/slack`: normalize Slack identity emoji aliases to `:emoji_name:` form, add optional `SlackSendIdentity` support to `createSlackDraftStream`, pass the resolved `slackIdentity` from `dispatchPreparedSlackMessage`, and add focused regression coverage for draft preview identity plus bare/pre-wrapped emoji handling.
