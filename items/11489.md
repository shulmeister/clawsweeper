## Summary

Keep #11489 open. Current main still supports DM/device pairing only; group handling remains explicit `open | allowlist | disabled` policy plus allowlists. The related #8495 and PR #42400 are adjacent but do not cover group pairing approval, owner notification, reject/approve CLI semantics, or pending read-only group-agent routing.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #11489 open for maintainer/product design. The likely path is an additive core/channel contract: add `groupPolicy: "pairing"` to config/schema/docs, persist pending group approvals keyed by channel/account/group ID, add CLI approve/reject semantics, emit owner-visible group-join or first-seen-unconfigured-group notifications, teach group-capable channels such as Telegram and WhatsApp to honor the state, and decide whether pending read-only observation belongs in the same change or a narrower follow-up coordinated with #8495 and PR #42400.
