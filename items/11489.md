## Summary

Keep #11489 open. Current main still does not implement group pairing, owner notification on new group add, approve/reject semantics for group chat IDs, or a pending-group reader mode. Related #8495 and #42400 are useful narrower context, but both are open and neither supersedes the full owner-controlled group pairing flow.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #11489 open for maintainer/product direction. The likely implementation is an additive core/channel contract change: extend group policy/config schema and docs, add pending group approval storage plus CLI approve/reject semantics, teach Telegram/WhatsApp group join or first-seen group paths to notify the owner, and decide whether pending read-only observation should be a separate never-reply-style mode or a new restricted runtime route.
