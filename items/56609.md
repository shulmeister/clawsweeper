## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still uses `.deleted.*` and `.reset.*` transcript archive names across implementation, tests, and docs, and it still removes archived/orphan transcript files during retention and disk-budget cleanup, so the requested rename/never-delete behavior is not implemented.

## Evidence

- **Archive naming is hard-coded as `deleted`/`reset`:** `SessionArchiveReason` only allows `"bak" | "reset" | "deleted"`, and archive artifact detection/parsing explicitly matches `.deleted.` and `.reset.` suffixes.
