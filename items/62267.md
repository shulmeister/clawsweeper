## Summary

Keep open. Current main has Ollama context-window controls, num_ctx forwarding, timeout/failover handling, and preemptive compaction, but I did not find source or maintainer evidence that the specific long-context Ollama prompt-ingestion latency/fallback behavior in #62267 is fixed. Related current reports also remain open, so this is not a safe implemented, duplicate, stale, or cannot-reproduce close.
