## Summary

Keep #39232 open. Current main still lacks a typed pre-filter raw inbound plugin hook, and the existing `message_received`/`before_dispatch` hooks run after routing, access, or channel gating rather than before channel-level filtering.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open for maintainer/API design. A good implementation would add an observe-only, privacy-aware pre-filter inbound contract to the typed plugin/channel SDK, wire bundled channels such as WhatsApp and Telegram before access and mention gating, document the privacy model, and cover the contract plus channel-specific ordering with tests. If maintainers decide raw ambient observation is out of scope, document that policy and the supported narrower alternatives.

## Risks / Open Questions

- A pre-filter raw observer would expose content that access control and mention gating currently suppress from plugins, so the design needs explicit opt-in, privacy documentation, and security review.
- The product shape is still a maintainer decision: a global typed plugin hook, per-channel observe configuration/sink, or a documented decision not to support ambient raw observation.
