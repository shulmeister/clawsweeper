## Summary

Keep open. Current main still documents and unit-tests Slack reaction system events, but I found no transport-level fix or reproduction proof that would justify closing this Socket Mode delivery report; the landed change cited in the changelog (#34045) was about multi-account session routing after receipt, not about Slack delivering `reaction_added`/`reaction_removed` over Socket Mode.
