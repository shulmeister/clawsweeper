## Summary

Keep #39117 open. Current main at 631552c554095b0c520376db9131dac0f5427f94 still lacks the PR's queued busy receipt, aborted embedded-run stale-reply suppression, per-session stuck-lane recovery, and richer sessions.reset audit metadata. Keep the PR open for maintainer judgment or author revision.
