## Summary

Current main implements the requested native exec allowlist capability, though the accepted contract is host-local exec approvals (`~/.openclaw/exec-approvals.json`) rather than a `tools.exec.allowlist` key in `openclaw.json`. The docs, schema, runtime enforcement, tests, and changelog all support closing as implemented.

## Evidence
