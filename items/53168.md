## Summary

Current main has adjacent fixes and regression coverage around CLI handshake timeouts, cron responsiveness, and degraded `gateway status` probing, but I could not verify from the code/tests alone that the exact `Telegram polling active + CLI WebSocket handshake timeout` report in #53168 is fully fixed end-to-end. The evidence is not strong enough for a conservative close.

## Evidence

- **CLI handshake mitigation exists on main:** CLI RPC commands flow through `src/cli/gateway-rpc.runtime.ts:6` into `callGateway`, and `src/gateway/call.ts:479` now yields with `setImmediate` before opening the WebSocket specifically to avoid `connect.challenge` handshake timeouts from event-loop starvation.
