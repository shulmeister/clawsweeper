## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement Slack App Home publishing: the Slack manifest/setup only enables the App Home Messages tab, does not subscribe to `app_home_opened`, and the runtime only registers message/reaction/member/channel/pin/interaction handlers with no `views.publish` path.
