## Summary

Keep open. Current main supports SecretRef sources for env, file, and exec, and documents a 1Password CLI recipe through exec, but it still does not provide native keyring or native 1Password provider sources requested by #17311. The prior issue discussion also reached this conclusion, and closing against the broader #13610 tracker would lose this narrower follow-through.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #17311 open until native keyring and 1Password SecretRef/provider sources are either implemented across types, schema, resolver, configure/setup UX, docs, and tests, or a maintainer explicitly decides that exec recipes are the final supported direction and opens/links a narrower canonical follow-up for any remaining keyring or macOS Keychain work.
