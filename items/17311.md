## Summary

Keep open. Current main supports SecretRef provider sources for env, file, and exec, and documents 1Password through a generic exec-provider recipe, but it still does not include native keyring or native 1Password SecretRef/provider sources requested by #17311.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #17311 open until native keyring and 1Password SecretRef/provider sources are implemented across config types, schema, resolver, configure/setup UX, docs, and tests, or until a maintainer explicitly decides that exec-based recipes are the final supported product direction and tracks any remaining macOS Keychain/keyring work in a narrower canonical issue.
