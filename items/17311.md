## Summary

Keep open. Current main supports SecretRef sources/providers for env, file, and exec, and documents 1Password through the generic exec provider, but it still lacks native keyring and 1Password provider sources requested by #17311.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #17311 open until native keyring and 1Password providers are implemented, or until a maintainer explicitly decides that exec-based recipes are the final product direction and opens/closes a narrower canonical follow-up for any remaining macOS Keychain/keyring work.
