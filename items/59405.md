## Summary

Evidence suggests the report may have been influenced by per-session model pinning and an older Codex model, but current `main` does not provide strong enough proof that issue #59405 is fully resolved or invalid. I would keep it open for a maintainer to confirm on a live Telegram session with the exact model/runtime combination or to narrow it into a model-selection bug instead of a Telegram-runtime bug.
