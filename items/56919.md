## Summary

Current main and the shipped v2026.4.23 release improve the skill-escape warning text and add bundled-skill tests, but I found no evidence that the repeated warning spam reported on v2026.3.28 was actually fixed. The loader still emits a warning each time an escaped candidate is encountered during discovery, so the report remains plausible and should stay open for maintainer judgment or a concrete repro on current builds.
