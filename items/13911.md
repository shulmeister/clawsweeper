## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still implements top-level subagent completion announce as an external delivery back to the requester route, and the shipped config/docs expose no per-channel suppression or reroute setting beyond behavioral `NO_REPLY` / `ANNOUNCE_SKIP` handling.
