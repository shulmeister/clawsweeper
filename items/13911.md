## Summary

Current main still does not provide per-channel sub-agent completion announce suppression or rerouting. The requester-channel delivery behavior remains documented, implemented, and covered by tests, and the related #69492 is still an open design proposal rather than shipped behavior.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #13911 open until OpenClaw ships a first-class subagent completion audience/delivery policy, likely in core config/schema/docs/tests, that can suppress or reroute completion announces per channel without relying on AGENTS.md behavior, sentinel tokens, or channel-specific outbound sanitizers. If #69492 lands a broader event-audience primitive, verify it covers the sessions_spawn completion path for Telegram/iMessage-style channels before closing this issue.
