## Summary

Keep #71563 open. Current main still routes Telegram `/new` and `/reset` through the ordinary chat/topic sequential key instead of the `:control` lane, while the PR's final patch narrowly adds that missing session-command bypass and removes the unsupported `/clear` case.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the PR open and either merge or adapt its narrow implementation in `extensions/telegram/src/sequential-key.ts`, preserving normal serialization for ordinary messages and keeping `/export-session` off the control lane while adding regression coverage for `/new`, `/reset`, and bot-username-suffixed commands.
