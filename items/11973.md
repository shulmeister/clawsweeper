## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still installs and documents a restart-only systemd unit; I found no `WatchdogSec`, `Type=notify`, `sd_notify`, or `NOTIFY_SOCKET` integration, and the existing gateway maintenance tick only broadcasts internal health/tick events.

## Evidence

- **Canonical systemd unit builder lacks watchdog/notify directives:** `buildSystemdUnit` emits `ExecStart`, `Restart=always`, `RestartSec=5`, `TimeoutStopSec=30`, `TimeoutStartSec=30`, `SuccessExitStatus=0 143`, and `KillMode=control-group`, with no `Type=notify` or `WatchdogSec` support.
