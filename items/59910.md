## Summary

Current main still keeps the generic inbound image cap at 6MB, but provider-bound session replay now sanitizes image blocks to Anthropic-safe 5MB defaults before model calls. The reported Slack/Anthropic 5-6MB rejection from 2026.2.6 is handled on main, even though provider-aware larger-image retention remains a separate enhancement area.
