## Summary

Keep open. Current main still has the reported behavior: `checkBrowserOrigin` compares `allowedOrigins` against `new URL(origin).origin`, which is `"null"` for opaque custom schemes such as `tauri://localhost`, so a specific allowlist entry will not match. The related PRs #46523 and #46555 proposed the right class of fix, but they were closed unmerged and the current source/tests do not contain that change.
