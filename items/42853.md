## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the proposed gateway/node heap-limit feature: there is no `gateway.nodeMaxOldSpaceMb` config surface, no `NODE_OPTIONS=--max-old-space-size=...` injection in supervised service environments, and no onboarding/setup prompt for this setting.
