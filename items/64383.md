## Summary

Keep open. Current main still uses the sandbox browser socat relay and CDP port-offset design that this issue asks maintainers to evaluate. The later code/docs/tests clarify why the relay currently exists, especially source-range restriction, but they do not remove or replace it with a direct CDP path.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this as the architectural tracking issue unless a maintainer makes an explicit product decision to retain the relay. A direct-CDP implementation would need to remove the port offset and socat startup from `scripts/sandbox-browser-entrypoint.sh`, drop the Dockerfile dependency if unused, update `src/agents/sandbox/browser.ts` orchestration and config/docs/tests, and preserve equivalent readiness, exposure, and access-control guarantees.
