Keep open. Current main contains related crash hardening and channel-health monitoring, but I did not find strong evidence that it fixes this recent gateway-wide webchat/WhatsApp wedge. The follow-up comments report similar behavior on v2026.4.22, including a config-read trigger, and current health-monitor behavior still looks heuristic rather than a direct fix for the reported mid-session silence.
