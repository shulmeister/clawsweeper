## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still implements session visibility and agent-to-agent scoping only at the global `tools.*` level; the per-agent `agents.list[].tools.sessions` / `agents.list[].tools.agentToAgent` overrides proposed in PR #59170 are not present in the shipped schema, types, runtime resolution, or docs.
