## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not provide a gateway-mediated way for `sessions_history` or `chat.history` to list or read archived `.jsonl.reset.*` transcripts. There is a bundled `session-logs` skill and a reset-archive fallback in the session-memory hook, but those are partial/workaround paths rather than the requested sandbox-safe archived-session recall surface.

## Evidence

- **sessions_history surface:** `sessions_history` only accepts `sessionKey`, `limit`, and `includeTools`, then forwards the resolved live session key to `chat.history`. There is no parameter or branch for archive selection, previous-session lookup, or reset-archive enumeration.
