## Summary

Current `main` already implements Mattermost auto-threaded channel replies and the `chatmode: "onmessage"` mention-gating fix. The feature shipped under `channels.mattermost.replyToMode` rather than a separate `replyInThread` boolean.

## Close Comment

Closing as implemented on `main` after Codex review. The requested Mattermost threading behavior is now available via `channels.mattermost.replyToMode` rather than a separate `replyInThread` boolean: `docs/channels/mattermost.md:140` documents `replyToMode: "all"` for channel/group replies, `extensions/mattermost/src/mattermost/monitor.ts:342` computes the effective thread root for top-level posts, and `extensions/mattermost/src/mattermost/monitor.ts:709` together with `extensions/mattermost/src/mattermost/send.ts:467` carries that reply target through outbound delivery. The `chatmode: "onmessage"` mention-gating problem is also fixed: `extensions/mattermost/src/mattermost/accounts.ts:66` maps `onmessage` to `requireMention = false`, and `extensions/mattermost/src/mattermost/monitor.test.ts:102` covers unmentioned channel posts and thread replies. This is already present in the shipped `v2026.4.22` release.
