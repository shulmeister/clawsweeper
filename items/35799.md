## Summary

Keep #35799 open. Current main has partial internal mitigation through stable per-agent heartbeat phasing and cron-expression top-of-hour staggering, but the requested public scheduler surface remains missing: no prime cadence mode, no configurable heartbeat phase/jitter controls, no resolved heartbeat phase/status exposure, and cron `every` remains a fixed interval without stagger/jitter support.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and turn it into a deliberate scheduler/API change. Preserve the shipped internal stable heartbeat phase, then design backward-compatible public controls for heartbeat phase/jitter and optional prime cadence. Extend cron `every` types, gateway protocol, normalizer, CLI, UI/status output, docs, and tests with explicit jitter/stagger semantics and resolved schedule observability.
