## Summary

Keep #35799 open. Current main partially mitigates heartbeat herding with a stable per-agent phase, but the requested product surface is still not implemented: no prime cadence mode, no exposed heartbeat phase-jitter controls, and no jitter/stagger support for cron `every` schedules. Cron staggering remains scoped to `cron` expressions, especially recurring top-of-hour schedules.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and either implement a backward-compatible scheduling surface for heartbeat and cron `every` jobs (`everyMode`/prime cadence if desired, deterministic phase jitter or explicit phase windows, schema/CLI/UI/status/docs/tests), or split the remaining scope into narrower maintainer-approved follow-ups while preserving the shipped stable heartbeat phase.
