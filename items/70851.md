## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement hiding internal sessions from the chat picker, and the PR itself is framed as an interim UI mitigation rather than a completed product fix. There is also an unresolved review concern that the proposed substring-based matcher could hide legitimate sessions with flexible keys, so conservative cleanup should not auto-close this PR.
