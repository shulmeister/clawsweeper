## Summary

Current main contains gateway-level channel supervision that catches Slack monitor failures, records them on the Slack account, limits channel restarts, and keeps other channels/gateway startup isolated. The linked PR #66342 correctly identified Slack auth errors as the trigger, but the reported gateway restart loop is already covered on main and in v2026.4.24.
