## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the requested behavior: chat bubbles are rendered directly in `ChatMessageBody` without an inner `ScrollView`, and the only scroll container in this surface is the outer transcript list. The issue’s proposed `.large`/`shouldTruncate` path also does not exist on current `main`, so there is no strong basis to close this as already fixed or obsolete.
