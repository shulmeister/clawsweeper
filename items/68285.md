## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` appears to have absorbed the reporter's workaround for broken tools/memory by adding `--skip-trust` to the bundled Gemini CLI backend, but there is no strong evidence in the repo that the reported Matrix-related 100% CPU regression is fixed or obsolete. The issue is recent, concrete, and still plausibly actionable.
