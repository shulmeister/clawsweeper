## Summary

Keep #15634 open. Current main has implemented several Mattermost parity pieces, including reactions, interactive buttons, typing indicators, threading, markdown-aware delivery, and media handling, but the remaining read receipts / presence scope is still not implemented or documented for Mattermost. The latest maintainer discussion also explicitly kept the umbrella open for that gap.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #15634 open as the Mattermost parity umbrella, or split the remaining read receipts / presence scope into a narrower Mattermost issue. Do not close it until that remaining behavior is implemented, documented, or explicitly descoped by a maintainer.
