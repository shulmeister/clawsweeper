## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still forces direct delivery for cron targets with a resolved `threadId`, and the cron delivery contract/docs/tests do not expose any opt-in `announce`/main-session reroute for topic/forum targets. This feature request is not implemented on `main` at `51186d2725439646f7fea0e59bb466f957404c46`.
