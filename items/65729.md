## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still intentionally keeps Mattermost DMs non-threaded, stores thread context only in an in-memory map, drops mention-only messages after normalization, and shows no WebSocket ping/keepalive implementation or thread-history backfill path. The issue is recent and the discussion points to unresolved product behavior rather than something already fixed.

## Evidence
