## Summary

The PR is internally contradictory: its title/body claim a `strictInlineEval` security-policy change in `src/node-host/invoke-system-run.ts`, but the actual PR commit/diff is unrelated secret test-support plumbing, and current `main` docs/tests still intentionally require explicit approval for strict inline eval even with `tools.exec.security="full"`.

## Evidence
