## Summary

Keep #6615 open. Current main at 82262387651ab270b565f71533ce4937c9c00dc6 still lacks first-class exec-approval denylist support: no `security: "denylist"`, no `ask: "on-match"`, no denylist storage, and no denylist match branch in system.run policy, Control UI, schema, or docs. The SafeClaw and CRE/claude-rule-enforcer links in the issue discussion are useful prior art, but they do not ship the requested OpenClaw core exec-approvals surface.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #6615 open until OpenClaw either implements first-class exec-approval denylist support across config/types/schema, approvals normalization/storage, gateway/node/system.run policy, CLI or Control UI, docs, and tests, or maintainers explicitly redirect this security-policy model to ClawHub/plugin policy and track any missing authorization hooks separately.
