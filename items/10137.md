## Summary

Keep open. Current main partially normalizes `openai-responses` requests (`input` array and `stream: true`) and has store/prompt-cache/reasoning policies, but the requested stable compat layer remains meaningfully incomplete: non-Codex Responses requests do not get a configurable non-empty top-level `instructions`, and `max_output_tokens` is still emitted when `maxTokens` is set. The linked #10140 appears to be the closed duplicate copy, while the duplicate note suggested consolidating discussion on #10137.
