## Summary

Current main has a Slack-specific solution and appears to fix the later Discord channel-bound-session repro, but the broader requested strict mention mode remains missing for Discord replies/threads and other implicit-reply channels. Since the issue asks for a way to disable implicit reply-to-bot mention bypass across affected channels, this should stay open rather than be closed as fully implemented.
