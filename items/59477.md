## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain the PR's change, and the remaining code/docs/tests show this area is still behaviorally unresolved rather than obsolete: sandboxed cross-agent `sessions_send` is blocked on `main` in the default spawned-clamp mode, while docs/tests also indicate sandboxed cross-agent access is only expected when `agents.defaults.sandbox.sessionToolsVisibility="all"` is explicitly allowed.
