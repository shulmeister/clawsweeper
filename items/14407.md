## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` implements password visibility toggles in the Control UI/web surface, but the native app surfaces are not covered across platforms. iOS and macOS still use `SecureField` without a reveal toggle, and Android gateway password fields have no visibility toggle wiring at all.
