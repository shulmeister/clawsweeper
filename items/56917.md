## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still filters owner-only tools solely by `senderIsOwner`, so configured `tools.alsoAllow` cannot re-expose `gateway` or other owner-only tools for non-owner external-channel sessions. The PR appears to address a real, still-unfixed gap rather than obsolete behavior.
