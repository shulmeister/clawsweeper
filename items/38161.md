## Summary

Current main still lacks #38161's central behavior: no postHookActions event queue/drain, no session-memory block/content save controls, no universal random suffix on memory filenames, and no disableTools/UUID hardening at the slug-generator call site. Related items are dependent or complementary, not replacements.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #38161 open for maintainer review or split it into narrower patches: slug-generation hardening with disableTools and UUID IDs, an additive optional postHookActions hook-event mechanism with drain tests, and session-memory block/content persistence controls with stronger filename uniqueness. Treat #38162 as dependent redirect-path work and #64181 as complementary slug-response validation, not replacements.
