## Summary

Keep open. Current main still preserves the parts of the report that matter most: tool cards are collapsed by default, the app passes `autoExpandToolCalls: false`, and the collapsed summary uses small 11-12px text with nowrap/ellipsis truncation. I did not find a canonical superseding item in the provided related context or local source search.
