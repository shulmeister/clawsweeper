## Summary

Keep open. Current main still lacks the PR's requested behavior: overflow recovery retries truncation whenever oversized tool results are detected, without checking whether tool-result reduction can plausibly clear the overflow. The PR also contains related fixes for attempt-local overflow estimates and context-overflow reset behavior that are not present on main. No close reason applies to this recently updated contributor PR.
