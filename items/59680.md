## Summary

Current main has superseding provider-aware failover classification for embedded/runtime assistant errors, and the related hardening shipped in v2026.4.24 via #70743. This PR's direct call-site conversion is no longer the best patch to land.

## Evidence

  - file: [src/agents/pi-embedded-helpers/errors.ts:3567](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/7bbd47349ec37f01a8f2b1dc219bf950615f361b/src/agents/pi-embedded-helpers/errors.ts#L3567)
