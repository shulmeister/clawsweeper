## Summary

Issue #35515 should close as a duplicate of the older open canonical tracker #9443 for prebuilt Android APK releases. Current main still does not ship official APK release assets, so the remaining product/release work should stay on #9443 rather than split across two issues.

## Best Possible Solution

Close #35515 as a duplicate and keep #9443 as the canonical open tracker until OpenClaw ships official Android APK release assets or a maintainer explicitly decides against APK distribution. Useful discussion from #35515, including signing/secrets help, AndroidForClaw as a community workaround, and F-Droid as a possible distribution target, should inform the canonical issue.
