## Summary

Keep open. Current main still does not wire the requested Baileys socket-level getMessage retry callback, cachedGroupMetadata callback, or group/LID update event handlers. It has partial related behavior, including cacheable signal keys, inbound-only group metadata lookup, and LID resolution through Baileys' signalRepository/auth files, but the concrete reliability work requested in #7433 remains actionable.
