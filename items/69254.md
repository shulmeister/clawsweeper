## Summary

Keep open. Current main has generic session-scoped restart-sentinel routing and Telegram session route persistence, but the evidence here does not prove Telegram direct gets the same post-restart recovery behavior as Control UI/WebChat end-to-end. The issue is recent, plausible, and reinforced by related open restart/reconnect reports.

## Evidence
