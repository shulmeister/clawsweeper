## Summary

Keep #61864 open. Current main has partial stale-token guards, but the specific invariant requested by the issue is still not guaranteed: a compaction-count update without `tokensAfter` can leave an old `totalTokens` snapshot marked fresh, and the next preflight compaction gate can still trust that persisted total instead of recomputing from the transcript.
