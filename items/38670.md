## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the PR's agent-scoped internal hook policy overrides: internal hook config is still global-only, agent/default config types and schemas do not expose `hooks`, and `triggerInternalHook` still has no config/agent-aware policy resolution path.

## Evidence
