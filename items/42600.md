## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does hydrate Telegram reply-parent context when Telegram includes `reply_to_message` or `external_reply`, but the normal gateway/session transcript lookup still reads only active transcript candidates and does not search `.reset` archives. That means the issue's reset/topic continuity failure is not clearly fixed on `main` from static inspection alone.

## Evidence

- **Telegram inbound path now hydrates reply-parent context:** `extensions/telegram/src/bot-message-context.session.ts:157-247` builds `replyTarget` from the inbound Telegram message, appends a `[Replying to ...]` or `[Quoting ...]` block to the body, and `extensions/telegram/src/bot-message-context.session.ts:322-345` stores `ReplyToId`, `ReplyToBody`, and `ReplyToSender` in the finalized inbound context.
