## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still exposes `last-heartbeat` as the latest in-memory heartbeat event, and exec completions still enqueue `reason: "exec-event"` wakes that the heartbeat runner can turn into exec-event prompts. That preserves the core mislabeling/contamination concern from #65161. At the same time, two sub-claims narrowed during review: current tests show scheduler hardening for cadence/retry paths, and `memory/heartbeat-state.json` is documented as a workspace bookkeeping template rather than a runtime-owned receipt file.
