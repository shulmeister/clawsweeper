## Summary

Keep open. The requested Telegram album/sendMediaGroup behavior is still missing on current main: mediaUrls are accepted, but Telegram delivery sends each media item individually. Related PR attempts are closed/unmerged, and the related issue is closed, so there is no canonical open item that clearly supersedes #13620.

## Evidence

- **Current Telegram media delivery is per-item:** deliverMediaReply iterates over params.mediaList one mediaUrl at a time, then dispatches individual sendAnimation/sendPhoto/sendVideo/sendVoice/sendDocument calls rather than building a sendMediaGroup request.
  - file: [extensions/telegram/src/bot/delivery.replies.ts:271](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/1dfa52d0711dfc86e1c04538fe46e3d43961766f/extensions/telegram/src/bot/delivery.replies.ts#L271)
