## Summary

Current main already has session maintenance/cleanup and requester-scoped subagent kill controls. The remaining direct session deletion and hard-kill semantics are tracked by the more specific open issues #35682 and #40840, so #12297 can close as superseded rather than remain a broad parallel tracker.

## Evidence

- **Cleanup command on main:** `openclaw sessions cleanup` is registered with `--dry-run`, `--enforce`, `--fix-missing`, `--active-key`, `--agent`, `--all-agents`, `--store`, and `--json`, covering the bulk cleanup workflow requested here.

  - file: [src/commands/sessions-cleanup.ts:298](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/1dfa52d0711dfc86e1c04538fe46e3d43961766f/src/commands/sessions-cleanup.ts#L298)
  - sha: [1dfa52d0711d](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/1dfa52d0711dfc86e1c04538fe46e3d43961766f)

  - sha: [1dfa52d0711d](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/1dfa52d0711dfc86e1c04538fe46e3d43961766f)

  - file: [src/agents/subagent-control.ts:161](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/1dfa52d0711dfc86e1c04538fe46e3d43961766f/src/agents/subagent-control.ts#L161)
