## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` (`60f7a59f5ed581839cfd1d52583449c5aa1f03a4`) still lacks the PR’s close-race handling: `src/gateway/call.ts` sets `ignoreClose` only after `await client.request(...)`, while `onClose` still immediately converts any pre-`ignoreClose` close into `formatGatewayCloseError(...)`. That means the reported response-vs-close race is not implemented on main. `src/gateway/client.ts` also still rejects pending requests before calling `onClose`, so the PR’s follow-up narrowing to benign `1000` closes remains relevant for preserving richer diagnostics on real disconnects.

## Evidence
