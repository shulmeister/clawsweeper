## Summary

Current main has the core seams needed for this use case: model-specific allow/deny already works through tools.byProvider keys that can be exact provider/model refs, and async human approval for arbitrary plugin-inspected tool calls is available through before_tool_call requireApproval. The remaining packaged policy of mapping model/tool pairs to approval prompts belongs as a plugin/ClawHub item rather than a new core tools.byModel namespace.
