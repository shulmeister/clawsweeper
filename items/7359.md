## Summary

Current main does not fully satisfy #7359. Slack DMs and channels remain separate session buckets, and cross-session recall is opt-in/visibility-gated rather than a unified default view of the agent's own Slack channel activity from a DM. The related closed issues cover thread context/queueing, not this broader DM-to-channel visibility request.
