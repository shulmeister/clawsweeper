## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still persists the full `skillsSnapshot.prompt` in session entries, serializes it into the session store, duplicates the same entry across isolated cron base/run keys, and carries it through reset and follow-up queue paths. I found no strong evidence that the reported retention/amplification bug is fixed or obsolete on `60f7a59f5ed581839cfd1d52583449c5aa1f03a4`.
