## Summary

Keep open. Current main still routes `/new` and `/reset` directly into session-reset behavior without a safety confirmation or configurable confirm-wipe setting. The related #45800 remains open, but it is not a full superseding canonical item because this issue also covers `/reset` and config behavior.
