## Summary

Keep #20786 open. Current main still only has Telegram Business update names in the allowed-update list; it does not register business_message/business_connection handlers, extend dedupe or sequential lanes for business payloads, persist or inject business_connection_id for outbound replies, or document/test Telegram Business support. The issue discussion already identifies PR #53629 as the related implementation attempt, but that work is not present in current main.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #20786 as the canonical Telegram Business support tracker. Land PR #53629 or an equivalent extensions/telegram implementation that registers business_connection and business_message handlers, routes business messages through the existing inbound pipeline, extends dedupe and sequential keys for business payloads, persists/maps business_connection_id for outbound replies, and adds focused tests plus user-facing Telegram docs.
