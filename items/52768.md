## Summary

Current main already has the WhatsApp listener-ownership and stale-listener recovery work needed for this report: outbound sends resolve the live listener from the gateway-owned controller, the controller registers/unregisters the active listener, stale sessions are forced into reconnect instead of remaining green, and the shipped changelog explicitly records fixes for missing active listeners and dead-listener watchdog recovery.
