## Summary

Keep #7338 open. Current main has provider-specific header and transport hooks, plus web_fetch User-Agent configuration, but not the requested opt-in agent attestation surface for arbitrary external HTTP requests, skills, or tools. The related #11137 was closed by a maintainer as a duplicate of #7338, making #7338 the canonical remaining tracker rather than a superseded item.
