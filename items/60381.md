## Summary

Keep open. Current main implements the evaluate action, but the requested click force parameter is still missing from the public act request type, tool schema, request normalization, and Playwright click dispatch. The related closed issue #55598 appears to cover the arbitrary-JavaScript/evaluate portion only, not the remaining force-click capability requested here.
