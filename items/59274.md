## Summary

Current `main` already contains targeted ACPX recovery for dead persistent sessions, retries stale resume paths without reusing dead session IDs, routes `openclaw acp` through the bridge-safe path, and ships a newer bundled `acpx` than the reported `0.4.0` environment.
