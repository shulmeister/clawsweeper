## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still lacks a WhatsApp read-only / `autoReply: false` style control, and the WhatsApp auto-reply path still sends every visible non-tool reply back to the inbound WhatsApp sender once that DM is admitted by `dmPolicy`/`allowFrom`.

## Evidence

- **WhatsApp config surface has no auto-reply suppression field:** `WhatsAppConfigSchema` and `WhatsAppAccountSchema` expose `dmPolicy`, `allowFrom`, `groupPolicy`, `replyToMode`, etc., but no channel/account/per-number `autoReply` or receive-only setting.
  - file: [src/config/zod-schema.providers-whatsapp.ts:51](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/272bd59e7a8a4d6ef6e03b9bdc1068a02986d56e/src/config/zod-schema.providers-whatsapp.ts#L51)
  - command: `nl -ba src/config/zod-schema.providers-whatsapp.ts | sed -n '1,220p'`
  - sha: [272bd59e7a8a](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/272bd59e7a8a4d6ef6e03b9bdc1068a02986d56e)
- **Docs only describe DM admission controls:** The WhatsApp docs describe `channels.whatsapp.dmPolicy` plus `allowFrom` for DM access, with no documented switch to accept inbound WhatsApp DMs without auto-replying on that surface.
  - file: [docs/channels/whatsapp.md:152](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/272bd59e7a8a4d6ef6e03b9bdc1068a02986d56e/docs/channels/whatsapp.md#L152)
