## Summary

Current main still appears to have the reported Feishu audio duration gap. `uploadFileFeishu` accepts and forwards `duration`, but `sendMediaFeishu` still uploads audio without computing or passing that field, so Feishu voice bubbles can still show 0 seconds.

## Evidence

- **uploadFileFeishu supports duration:** `uploadFileFeishu` defines `duration?: number` and includes it in `client.im.file.create` data when provided.
  - file: [extensions/feishu/src/media.ts:389](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/4069c81b159087cb71f6b64676d533cf0d897d68/extensions/feishu/src/media.ts#L389)
  - command: `nl -ba extensions/feishu/src/media.ts | sed -n '389,755p'`
  - sha: [4069c81b1590](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/4069c81b159087cb71f6b64676d533cf0d897d68)
- **sendMediaFeishu omits duration:** `sendMediaFeishu` calls `uploadFileFeishu` with `cfg`, `file`, `fileName`, `fileType`, and `accountId`; no duration is computed or forwarded for `routing.msgType === "audio"`.
  - file: [extensions/feishu/src/media.ts:749](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/4069c81b159087cb71f6b64676d533cf0d897d68/extensions/feishu/src/media.ts#L749)
