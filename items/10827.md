## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has partial skill hardening, but not the requested provenance tracking or permission-manifest/runtime-enforcement model. The repo still documents third-party skills as untrusted, direct ClawHub skill installs still download into the workspace, and the threat model still calls out ClawHub supply-chain limits plus pending sandboxing/audit work. One sub-claim in the report is overstated: SKILL bodies are not eagerly inlined into the prompt; they are listed and read on demand.
