## Summary

Current `main` still limits the Claude name-based image-capability fallback to `claude-cli`, not direct `anthropic`. Gateway chat/agent paths rely on that helper to decide whether pasted images can stay inline, so the PR is still addressing a live bug rather than obsolete or already-shipped behavior.
