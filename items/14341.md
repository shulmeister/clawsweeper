## Summary

Current main implements the Feishu/Lark outbound media path requested by #14341: images route as Feishu image messages, native Ogg/Opus audio routes as Feishu audio messages, voice-intent audio can be transcoded to the required Ogg/Opus format, and docs/tests cover the behavior.
