## Summary

Current `main` already implements the custom-provider image-tool path: config providers with image-capable models are auto-registered for media understanding, `models.json` is materialized from `openclaw.json` before image-model lookup, and explicit `agents.defaults.imageModel` routing was fixed on `main` in commit `d268c850e61a55422ea335f5fbf8154a1fcf24ce`.
