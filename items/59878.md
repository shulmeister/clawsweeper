## Summary

Keep open. The report is recent, concrete, and describes a high-impact stale session state that was not directly proven fixed on current main. Related timeline evidence includes an open, similar persisted-session wedge issue (#60542), and the available release-note evidence only points to narrower restart/stale-lock recovery, not the broader sessions.abort no-active-run cleanup requested here.
