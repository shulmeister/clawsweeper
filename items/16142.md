## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still handles main-session cron reminders by enqueueing a system event, building a natural-language `cron-event` prompt, and invoking `getReplyFromConfig`; duplicate suppression and event draining happen after the model call or on later runs, so they do not solve the reported OpenAI/API-cost problem. The related PR #15403 is closed unmerged and unrelated to this cron/heartbeat formatting path.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #16142 open until main-session cron reminders can bypass `getReplyFromConfig` for simple relay/formatting cases, or until maintainers explicitly decide model-mediated cron reminder delivery is intentional. A good fix would likely live around `executeMainSessionCronJob`, `resolveHeartbeatRunPrompt`, or a small deterministic delivery helper, with regression tests proving simple reminders do not call the model while agentic cron jobs still can.
