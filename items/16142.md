## Summary

Keep #16142 open. Current main still handles main-session cron reminders by enqueuing a cron-tagged system event, turning it into a natural-language `cron-event` prompt, and calling `getReplyFromConfig`; existing duplicate/drain logic does not avoid the model call that drives the reported OpenAI/API-cost issue.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #16142 open until main-session cron reminders can bypass `getReplyFromConfig` for simple relay/formatting cases, or until maintainers explicitly decide model-mediated cron reminder delivery is intentional. A likely implementation belongs around `executeMainSessionCronJob`, `resolveHeartbeatRunPrompt`, or a small deterministic delivery helper, with regression tests proving simple reminders do not call the model while agentic cron jobs still can.
