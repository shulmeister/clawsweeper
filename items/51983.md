## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement this PR, and the current code/docs/tests now explicitly scope OpenRouter `cache_control` injection to Anthropic model refs on verified OpenRouter routes. That is strong evidence against `implemented_on_main`, but not strong enough to justify closing this PR under `cannot_reproduce` without a live reproduction or upstream maintainer decision that the Anthropic-only policy is final.
