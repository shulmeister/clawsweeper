## Summary

Current main implements the underlying toolResult.details persistence hardening through an always-on bounded-details sanitizer rather than the opt-in policy from the PR branch. It avoids the JSON.stringify undefined crash path, bounds oversized details without full serialization, reapplies final caps after hooks, and has targeted tests and docs on main.
