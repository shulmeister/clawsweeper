## Summary

Current `main` has adjacent memory safety pieces, but not the end-to-end write resolver described in #42648. Durable promotion in `memory-core` still filters/ranks candidates and then appends them to `MEMORY.md`; it does not branch into create/merge/update/conflict-for-review or keep a revision trail. `memory-wiki` can surface contradictions, provenance gaps, and low-confidence claims, but that is review/reporting around wiki pages, not the requested candidate-memory ingestion pipeline.
