## Summary

Keep open. Current main still appears to route macOS `camera.snap` with `--device-id` to the matched AVFoundation device before applying the requested `front`/`back` facing, which matches the reported Continuity Camera failure mode. The related allowlist issues #58158/#58159 address whether `camera.snap` is advertised/allowed on macOS, not this front-vs-rear capture behavior.

## Evidence
