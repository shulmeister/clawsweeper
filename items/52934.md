## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still routes bare npm-safe plugin install specs through ClawHub first, documents that behavior as intentional since `2026.3.22`, and only warns on non-official ClawHub channels rather than enforcing a trust-bound mapping. That means the reported name-squatting concern is not clearly fixed or obsolete.

## Evidence
