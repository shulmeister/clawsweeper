## Summary

Current `main` already addresses the reported macOS LaunchAgent false positive by canonicalizing gateway service entrypoints, preferring `dist/index.js` over legacy `dist/entry.js`, and covering the repair path in tests. The fix is documented as shipped in `v2026.4.23`.

## Evidence
