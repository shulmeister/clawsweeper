## Summary

Keep open. Current main has partial Discord reconnect-zombie mitigation, but the issue's core maintainer-cleanup ask is not clearly implemented: the lifecycle now force-stops sockets that open and never become READY again, yet Discord still does not publish transport-activity health signals and the generic health monitor still only rate-limits restarts instead of escalating recovery across provider lifecycles.

## Evidence
