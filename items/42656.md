## Summary

Keep open: current `main` does not implement the PR's requested Synology Chat behavior. The webhook handler still strips `trigger_word` unconditionally, the Synology Chat config/account types do not expose `keywordFilterEnabled`, and current docs/tests still describe and assert the stripping-only behavior.
