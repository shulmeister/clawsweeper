## Summary

Current main already resolves WhatsApp inbound access per account on every message, silently blocks unauthorized DMs in allowlist mode, and drops self-chat outbound echoes before they can recurse back into inbound handling.

## Evidence

- **Allowlist mode blocks without pairing reply:** `checkInboundAccessControl` resolves the WhatsApp inbound policy from config/account, skips pairing-store approvals when `dmPolicy === "allowlist"`, and only sends a reply on the explicit `pairing` decision; other unauthorized DM decisions return blocked without `sendMessage`.
