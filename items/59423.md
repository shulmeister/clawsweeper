## Summary

Current `main` already covers the core-safe parts of this idea with built-in tool-result truncation and session pruning, while OpenClaw’s documented architecture now pushes richer context-compaction strategies into context-engine/plugins. This PR’s bespoke legacy-core compression stack is therefore out of scope for core, and the PR discussion also identified unresolved correctness/integration problems that make it a poor candidate to keep around in core form.
