## Summary

Current `main` still injects subagent completion text into the parent/internal task-completion event without any dedicated `maxSubagentResultChars` limit. OpenClaw now caps stored `frozenResultText` at 100KB, but that is a much larger safeguard than the issue requests and it does not add a configurable per-injection truncation path analogous to tool-result truncation.

## Evidence

- **Subagent announce still passes raw findings through:** `subagent-announce.ts` still builds `findings` directly from `childCompletionFindings || reply || "(no output)"` with no truncation or config lookup before attaching it to the internal `task_completion` event.
