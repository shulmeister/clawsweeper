## Summary

Keep open. Current main mitigates parts of the old failure mode with server-side chat.send ACK/idempotency and draft/error restoration, and the related WebChat reconnect history/event gap work is present. But the requested client-side outbound queue/replay is still missing: disconnected sends are not enqueued, pending WebSocket RPCs are rejected on close, and the existing chatQueue is only for active-run deferral/steering rather than reconnect delivery.

## Evidence
