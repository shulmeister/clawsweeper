## Summary

Not strong enough to close. Current main does not reference or load `skill.json`, and it now has a documented `SKILL.md`/frontmatter-based skill flow with implemented tool-dispatch for slash commands, but the report still points at a real gap between unsupported `skill.json` expectations and at least one remaining doc sentence that implies custom tool definitions in frontmatter.
