## Summary

Keep #13387 open. The linked PR #13388 was closed unmerged, and current main still does not implement the requested model-context pruning of consecutive assistant NO_REPLY transcript messages. Main has adjacent mitigations for silent-token delivery/display and heartbeat-pair filtering, but those do not collapse repeated NO_REPLY assistant turns before replaying session history to the model.
