## Summary

Keep open. Current main has useful Feishu mitigations like streaming cards, textChunkLimit, and API-call reduction flags, but the requested configurable minimum interval/outbound rate limiting is still missing. Non-streaming chunked Feishu replies are still sent sequentially without a pacing hook.
