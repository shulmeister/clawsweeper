## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` already fixes the `@everyone`/`@here` part of the report, but role mentions are still not treated as explicit mentions for Discord mention-gated agents. The inbound preflight path only marks direct user mentions as explicit and does not look up the bot's guild role memberships before deciding `wasMentioned`.

## Evidence
