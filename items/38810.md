## Summary

Current `main` already fixes the reported Podman launcher bug: `scripts/run-openclaw-podman.sh` loads `~/.openclaw/.env` before deriving `GATEWAY_BIND`, then passes that resolved value to `node dist/index.js gateway --bind ...`. The fix is also documented and recorded in the changelog as shipped in 2026.3.7.
