## Summary

Keep open. Current main still routes `/hooks/agent` and mapped agent hooks through isolated cron-agent turns, and isolated turns still force a new session id even when the caller supplies a stable `sessionKey`. The current hook config/types/schema also do not expose the proposed `sessionMode` or equivalent persistent-session option, and the related fix PRs in the provided issue context were closed unmerged.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue open until OpenClaw implements an explicit persistent hook-session path, likely by adding a backward-compatible `sessionMode: "isolated" | "persistent"` or equivalent hook mapping/request option, threading it through hook normalization/schema/docs/tests, and making persistent hook runs call `resolveCronSession` without `forceNew` while preserving fresh isolated behavior for existing hooks and cron jobs.
