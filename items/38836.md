## Summary

Keep open. Current main has partial restart resilience, including SIGUSR1 drain, restart sentinel delivery, restart continuation queues, and main-session recovery, but it still does not implement the requested native notification to Feishu users whose in-flight sessions are interrupted by a plain supervisor/systemctl restart or by an aborted recovery path. The remaining gap is specifically external user-visible notification/recovery across all affected sessions, not just one requested restart sentinel session.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep tracking this issue until OpenClaw either implements native restart interruption handling for external channel sessions or a maintainer explicitly decides the product boundary. A solid fix would persist per-affected-session restart notices or resumable continuations with deliveryContext during shutdown/drain aborts, recover them on startup, deliver visible Feishu/channel notifications or resumed replies, and cover both SIGUSR1/openclaw restart and supervisor SIGTERM restart behavior with generic channel plus Feishu-oriented regression tests.
