## Summary

Keep #38836 open. Current main has better managed-restart drain behavior, restart intent, per-session restart sentinel delivery, and internal main-session recovery, but it still does not provide the issue's requested Feishu-visible notification/fan-out for all in-flight Feishu sessions interrupted by a direct `systemctl --user restart openclaw-gateway` path.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open until OpenClaw either implements generic external-channel restart interruption handling or explicitly documents direct supervisor/systemctl restarts as outside the supported no-loss boundary. The likely fix belongs in gateway restart/session recovery: persist affected delivery contexts before shutdown or while marking aborted sessions, then on startup send a Feishu-visible interruption notice or resume-and-deliver through the original route with regression coverage for Feishu or a generic channel delivery contract.

## Risks / Open Questions

- A future implementation needs duplicate-notice protection when a final Feishu reply was delivered just before shutdown or a restart sentinel already posted a routed notice.
- Recovery must preserve enough Feishu route metadata, including account, chat, thread/reply target, and session key, so notices land in the original conversation.
