## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has stable/beta/dev channels and a beta-first promotion flow, but it does not implement the requested fixed monthly stable cadence, optional weekly stable program, or a documented one-month stable support commitment. The release workflows are still manual, and the changelog shows multiple stable releases within April 2026 rather than a monthly stable train.

## Evidence

- **Public release policy defines channels, not a monthly schedule:** `docs/reference/RELEASING.md` documents `stable`, `beta`, and `dev`, plus a beta-first flow where stable follows after the latest beta is validated.
