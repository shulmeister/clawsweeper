The PR's broader English stopword list was not merged verbatim, but the observable memory-dreaming noise path is covered on main.

Close #68473 and keep the shipped #70737 implementation. It solves the reported #68449 dreaming-noise path with targeted concept filtering, REM legacy-tag filtering, and corpus-level cron/heartbeat cleanup; broader configurable stopword work should be tracked separately if still desired.
