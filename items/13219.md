## Summary

Current main still relies on transcript/session-store scanning and Gateway summaries for usage visibility. The requested native append-only per-call usage ledger with source/session/model attribution is not implemented, and the related open PRs are either transcript-backed summaries (#49064) or a narrower aggregation bugfix (#70205), not a replacement for this issue.
