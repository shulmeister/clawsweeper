## Summary

Keep open. Current main has an internal `argPattern` field, but `matchAllowlist` explicitly limits argument-pattern matching to Windows. On non-Windows platforms, including the issue's `/opt/homebrew/bin/node` and `/Users/...` example, the first matching binary path still satisfies the allowlist regardless of arguments, so the requested narrow script-level authorization remains missing.
