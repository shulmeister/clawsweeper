## Summary

Keep open. Current main has useful mitigation for dotenv-backed systemd values, but the reported forced reinstall problem is not fully resolved: existing/provider secrets such as OPENAI_API_KEY can still be carried forward into the generated service environment, non-dotenv values are still rendered inline as Environment= lines, and reinstall still copies the previous unit to .bak before overwriting. Related #64046 and #69066 are complementary broader discussions, not a canonical superseding fix for this concrete installer behavior.
