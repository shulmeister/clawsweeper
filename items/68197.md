## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has the exact LINE non-text fail-open mention-gating behavior described by the PR, and the proposed option is not present in shipped code. This PR is a scoped, plausible LINE-extension change rather than something already implemented, unreproducible, ClawHub-scoped, or incoherent.

## Evidence

- **Current LINE handler still fails open for non-text group messages:** On `main`, LINE group handling sets `requireMention` from group config, but passes `canDetectMention: message.type === "text"` into the shared mention gate. Non-text messages therefore bypass mention gating even when `requireMention` is enabled.
