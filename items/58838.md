## Summary

Keep open. Current main still retries compaction summary generation per chunk with `retryAsync({ attempts: 3 })` and has no active circuit-breaker state or shared failure budget. The two linked fix PRs (#58846 and #58857) were closed unmerged, and their review discussion called out that the proposed circuit breaker was unreachable/dead code, so the issue is still actionable.
