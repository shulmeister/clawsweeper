Keep open: current `main` still has the `nodes status`/`nodes list` backend split described in the report, and the aligning fix exists only in open PR #65772, not on `main` or in `v2026.4.23`.

So `nodes status` is reading a broader effective catalog than `nodes list` is.
  - file: [src/gateway/server-methods/nodes.ts:697](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/c150110e02550eaf6c56db0c851dc62fc0ca88e1/src/gateway/server-methods/nodes.ts#L697)
