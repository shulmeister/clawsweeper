## Summary

Current main already implements the health-monitor behavior needed here: stale-socket recovery is based on provider-proven transport activity, Telegram polling publishes transport activity on successful polls, and the monitor restart path is channel-scoped rather than a full gateway bind path. This was shipped in v2026.4.23.

## Evidence
