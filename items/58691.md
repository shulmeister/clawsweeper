## Summary

Current `main` already implements the requested behavior: `tools.exec.security` / `tools.exec.ask` are used as the fallback policy when host approvals omit those fields, gateway/node exec defaults are now `full` + `off`, and docs/tests explicitly cover the merged-policy behavior that replaces the 2026.3.31 regression.

## Evidence
