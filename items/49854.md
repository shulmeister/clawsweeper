## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still reproduces the reported leak path: `normalizeReplyPayload` strips a trailing `NO_REPLY` token from text-only replies and keeps any preceding text, so a payload like `"not directed at me. NO_REPLY"` would still normalize to visible channel text instead of being suppressed. The proposed PR also appears to conflict with current expected behavior for substantive trailing-`NO_REPLY` replies and with directive parsing that happens later in the pipeline.

## Evidence

For a text-only payload like `not directed at me. NO_REPLY`, the stripped text remains non-empty and would be returned.
