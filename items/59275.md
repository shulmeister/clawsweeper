## Summary

Current `main` already covers the reported WhatsApp reconnect-loss case: outbound reply/send paths use the live socket reference, retry transient disconnect errors with backoff, and regression tests cover reconnect-time text/media retry behavior. The changelog records this reconnect-gap delivery fix as shipped in 2026.4.10.
