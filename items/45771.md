## Summary

Keep open. Current main has useful provider usage visibility, including Anthropic 5h/week windows surfaced through status and session_status, but it does not implement the requested pace-aware feature: no projected pace tier, no pace config, no automatic autonomous/subagent throttling, and no capture of Anthropic ratelimit headers from existing inference responses. The issue discussion's provider-aware caution remains relevant, and the related open rate-limiting items do not fully supersede this Anthropic pace/status/header proposal.
