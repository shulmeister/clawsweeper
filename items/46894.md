## Summary

Current main still appears to have the reported bug. The Control UI `/model` executor treats only a bare `/model` as informational; any non-empty argument, including `status` or `list`, is trimmed and sent to `sessions.patch` as the requested model. The linked PRs #46895 and #47069 are relevant proposed fixes, but they are open/unmerged in the provided context and the fix is not present on main, so this should stay open.
