## Summary

Keep #9797 open. Current main still lacks an agent-facing `queue_status` tool or a stable queue snapshot surface that exposes per-lane queue depth, active runs, max concurrency, and per-agent workload. The related #43618 discussion is useful and aligns with the operator-facing angle, but that PR is open and explicitly scopes out the in-agent `queue_status` tool requested here.
