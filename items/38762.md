## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still treats `browser action=act` with a top-level `ref` plus `request={...}` as invalid because the executor returns `params.request` verbatim instead of merging legacy top-level act fields. The misleading browser-service wording looks partially fixed on `main`, but the underlying ref-placement inconsistency reported in #38762 remains.
