## Summary

Keep open. Current main does not implement a cron-scoped `tools.cron.tools.deny` policy or make cron inherit `tools.subagents.tools.deny`; cron has a per-delivery message-tool policy and per-job `toolsAllow`, but the requested system-wide cron denylist remains missing.

## Evidence
