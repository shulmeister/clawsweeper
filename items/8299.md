## Summary

Keep open. Current main still does not expose the requested framework-level sub-agent announce suppression via config or a documented sessions_spawn parameter. The related PRs were closed unmerged, and the remaining open related item (#44309) is adjacent A2A dispatch work rather than a canonical replacement for this exact request.
