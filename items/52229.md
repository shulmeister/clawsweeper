## Summary

Current `main` has moved away from channel-branded BlueBubbles helper seams. The bundled BlueBubbles plugin now gets pairing, reply, and webhook helpers from documented generic SDK subpaths through its own `runtime-api.ts`/`monitor-processing-api.ts`, so this PR's extra `openclaw/plugin-sdk/bluebubbles` exports are obsolete rather than missing functionality on `main`.
