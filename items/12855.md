## Summary

Keep open. Current main has a useful package-install auto-updater under `update.auto.*`, shipped in the 2026.2.22 changelog, but it does not implement several meaningful parts of #12855: confirmation/notify-only modes, HH:MM or cron scheduling with timezone, or configured post-update channel notification. The linked #65011 is a good UX/discoverability follow-up, but it does not fully supersede the remaining confirmation and notification workflow requested here.
