## Summary

Keep open. Current main has partial safety mechanisms (workspace bootstrap injection, exec/ACP permission modes, and plugin before_tool_call approval hooks), but they do not implement the requested higher-priority behavioral rule layer or general confirmation gate for ambiguous user questions, commits/deployments, ticket creation, or other non-shell actions. The issue is recent, detailed, and still describes meaningful core/harness behavior rather than an obsolete or ClawHub-only request.
