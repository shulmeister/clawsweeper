## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `564f820efa2bb81ae49b8f55a93e41492ad31917` still drops Feishu card callback fields `action.option`, `action.options`, and `action.form_value`, so this is neither implemented on main nor obsolete.

## Evidence
