## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` partially supersedes the startup-latency motivation with a safer secrets-runtime fast path and already-parallel startup maintenance, but this PR as submitted still carries unresolved correctness/security regressions and is not implemented on `main` in its proposed form.

## Evidence
