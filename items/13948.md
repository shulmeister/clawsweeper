## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still applies `tools.allow`/`tools.deny` at whole-tool granularity, not per-action granularity, and the documented channel action toggles do not fully cover the requested `message:send` vs `message:read` split. In particular, Slack and Discord still group read/send/edit/delete together under `messages`, so the reported policy gap remains.
