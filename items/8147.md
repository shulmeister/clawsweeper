## Summary

Do not close #8147. Current main partially protects IME composition by ignoring Enter while `isComposing`/keyCode 229 is active, but WebChat still submits on plain Enter and there is no configurable Cmd/Ctrl+Enter send-hotkey setting in the WebChat config, types, docs, or render props. This remains a concrete enhancement request rather than an implemented, duplicate, stale, or ClawHub-scoped item.
