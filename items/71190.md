## Summary

Current main does not provide a high-confidence fix for #71190. The orphan repair path still prepends the full extracted orphan text into the next prompt without stripping earlier queued markers or aborted-run notes, so an already-accumulated orphan block can still be merged again on a later turn. The related items are adjacent queue/abort bugs, but they do not supersede this specific collect-mode snowball report.

## Evidence
