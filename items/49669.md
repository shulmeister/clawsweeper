## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still lacks lazy-import failure handling in `ui/src/ui/app-render.ts`, so the reported blank-view failure mode remains plausible, but the PR's exact `.catch()` approach still carries the reviewed infinite-retry-loop risk and does not have strong evidence for safe closure.

## Evidence

- **Current main still has no rejection handling in `createLazy()`:** At `ui/src/ui/app-render.ts:161-173`, `createLazy()` only chains `.then(...)` on `loader()` and never attaches a `.catch(...)` or resets state after a rejected import promise.
  - file: [ui/src/ui/app-render.ts:161](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/320d52a23e41a326287826219a20f1f29f334554/ui/src/ui/app-render.ts#L161)

  - sha: [320d52a23e41](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/320d52a23e41a326287826219a20f1f29f334554)
