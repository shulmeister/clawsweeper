## Summary

Keep #7722 open. Current main still only exposes the narrower `tools.fs.workspaceOnly` boolean; it does not accept `tools.fileAccess`, `mode`, `allowedPaths`, or `denyPaths`, and exec is not governed by a filesystem allow/deny policy. Related PR #60981 remains open and explicitly leaves arbitrary shell execution out of scope.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #7722 open until OpenClaw implements or explicitly rejects a versioned filesystem access policy, likely under `tools.fs` or a successor config contract, with canonicalized deny-wins `allowedPaths`/`denyPaths` semantics enforced across file/media/apply_patch paths and a clear exec strategy. Treat `tools.fs.workspaceOnly`, #5948, #8719, and PR #60981 as related partial/contextual work rather than completion.
