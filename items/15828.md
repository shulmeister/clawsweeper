## Summary

Keep #15828 open. Current main still leaves bundled session-memory as a command-only hook for /new and /reset. Daily/idle session lifecycle hooks exist, but they do not invoke the bundled hook; shutdown triggers, periodic/message-count checkpoints, and LLM summary output also remain unimplemented. A maintainer Codex review comment on the issue already reached the same keep-open conclusion, and the related open PRs only cover the narrower daily/idle rollover portion.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #15828 open unless maintainers intentionally split it. The product path should either extend bundled session-memory with configurable idle/shutdown/message-count triggers, dedupe, summary-generation policy, config/docs updates, and focused tests, or split daily/idle rollover work into #61675/#71792 while leaving shutdown, periodic checkpoints, and summary output tracked here or in explicit follow-ups.
