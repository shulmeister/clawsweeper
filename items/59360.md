## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still builds the Feishu card footer as `Agent: ... | Model: ... | Provider: ...`, injects that footer through Feishu markdown card elements, and exposes no Feishu config knob to disable or reformat the note. The issue discussion usefully narrowed the likely fix direction, but that proposal is not implemented on `main` yet.
