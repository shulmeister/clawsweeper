## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still fail-closes on Mattermost slash-command registration when `GET /commands` errors, so the PR's claimed 403 recovery and restart-safe cache behavior are not implemented on `main` as of `320d52a23e41a326287826219a20f1f29f334554`.
