## Summary

This is a Feishu/Lark cross-bot workflow request, not evidence of a current OpenClaw core bug. The documented Feishu channel handles normal user-origin messages through Feishu event subscription, while the related discussion in #43563 identifies bot-origin delivery as a Feishu platform limitation. There is already a ClawHub skill, FeiShu Robot @ Protocol, that implements an opt-in protocol/workaround for multi-robot identity and replies.

## Evidence
