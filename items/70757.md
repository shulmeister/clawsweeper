## Summary

Keep #70757 open. Current main still authenticates BlueBubbles inbound webhooks with `channels.bluebubbles.password`, still documents putting that password in the public webhook URL, and lacks the PR's dedicated `webhookSecret` config/setup/test coverage. The linked PR remains a live, relevant fix for #70755 rather than an obsolete or superseded item.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #70757 open. The best maintainer path is to review, adapt, and land the dedicated `channels.bluebubbles.webhookSecret` fix: authenticate inbound BlueBubbles webhooks against that separate secret, reject missing or password-reused webhook secrets in schema/setup, expose the noninteractive setup flag, update secret registry/generated metadata/docs, and close #70755 once merged and validated.
