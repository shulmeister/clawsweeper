## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not include the requested built-in aliases, and the PR discussion surfaces a real unresolved correctness risk around `/m` model switching that is not obviously handled by the current command/directive plumbing.

## Evidence

- **Current main alias registry:** `buildBuiltinChatCommands()` on `main` still only adds the older aliases (`/id`, `/thinking`/`/t`, `/v`, `/reason`, `/elev`, `/tell`). None of `/c`, `/ctx`, `/s`, `/n`, `/m`, `/ms`, `/h`, `/f`, or `/u` are registered.
