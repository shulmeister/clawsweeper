## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has a docs/i18n policy and existing Japanese UI localization, but nothing in the repo implements or resolves the specific proposal about `openclaw.co.jp` ownership, a Japanese community hub, or whether the core team wants to endorse that external domain. This is still a plausible product/community request, so the evidence is not strong enough for a cleanup close.
