## Summary

Keep #69513 open. Current main still validates cron announce delivery channels but not `delivery.accountId`; the CLI/protocol/normalizer accept any non-empty accountId and the isolated delivery target gives an explicit job accountId highest precedence. The linked OpenClaw PR #69518 proposed the right class of fix, but it is closed and unmerged, and its head commit is not present in this checkout.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and implement the #69518-style fix in `src/gateway/server-methods/cron.ts`: validate `delivery.accountId` and announce-mode `delivery.failureDestination.accountId` during `cron.add` and `cron.update` against the configured accounts for the normalized delivery channel, while preserving flat single-account configs and omitted accountId behavior. Add focused accept/reject coverage in the gateway cron validation tests and a user-facing changelog entry when the fix lands.
