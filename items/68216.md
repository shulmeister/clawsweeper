## Summary

Keep open. I do not have high-confidence evidence that `main` fixes this bug: the issue-linked PR `#68366` proposing `--yolo` was closed, not merged, and Gemini CLI’s current upstream docs still describe `--yolo` / `--approval-mode` as the tool-approval controls for non-interactive runs rather than `--skip-trust`. ([github.com](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/68366))
