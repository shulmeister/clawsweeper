## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain the PR's route-scoped embedded dedupe wiring: embedded block-reply suppression still short-circuits on normalized text alone, and the embedded message-send tracker still records only a single explicit `to/target` with a required/fallback provider instead of preserving full route context.

## Evidence
