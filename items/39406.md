## Summary

Keep #39406 open. Current main still lacks the requested normal-chat `toolErrorDelivery`/`all|final|off` policy or global config that suppresses mutating tool-error warnings after a successful final reply. Main has partial same-action retry cleanup and a per-run `suppressToolErrorWarnings` hook, but tests still encode visible mutating warnings even when `messages.suppressToolErrors` is enabled and even after assistant output.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #39406 open for a documented normal-chat tool-error delivery policy near `resolveToolErrorWarningPolicy` and `buildEmbeddedRunPayloads`. The best fix should preserve the safe default, add config/schema/help/docs coverage for the chosen policy, keep unresolved mutating failures visible unless explicitly suppressed, and coordinate with #39631 and #51091 without treating unmerged PRs as shipped behavior.
