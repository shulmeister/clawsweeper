## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain the PR's Telegram group-binding allowlist bypass or the Telegram-only restart drain delay, and `src/channels/chat-type.ts` still does not normalize `"supergroup"`. One part of the report is weaker on current `main` because Telegram routing now supplies `peer.kind: "group"` directly from the extension layer, but that is not enough to treat the PR as implemented or obsolete. The PR discussion also does not supply a close reason: the only review concern shown in the timeline was the channel-agnostic delay, and the author addressed that in the follow-up commit.
