## Summary

Keep open. Current main does not implement iMessage auto-acknowledgment or native typing lifecycle wiring. The iMessage config schema/types have no autoAck or typing field, and the inbound reply path creates the reply pipeline without typing callbacks before dispatching to the agent. The discussion note about imsg v0.5.0 adding typing support is useful, but OpenClaw has not wired that into the iMessage plugin, and the related #4065 is closed rather than an open canonical tracker.
