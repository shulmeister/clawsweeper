## Summary

The reported reply-session model drift is already addressed on `main` and in the shipped `v2026.4.23` release: fallback success is stored as a transient auto override, and the next turn explicitly clears that override and retries the configured primary model.

## Evidence

- **Fallback success is stored as transient auto selection:** In the `v2026.4.23` tree, the reply fallback path writes fallback state with `modelOverrideSource: "auto"`, which marks the fallback as transient instead of a permanent user-selected model.
