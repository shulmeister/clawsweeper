## Summary

Keep open. Current main still exposes `before_tool_call`/`after_tool_call` through plugin hooks, but not through the HOOK.md/internal hooks event system requested by #13364. The related discussion also supports keeping this as the canonical open request: #17008 was closed as a duplicate of this issue, and commenters added concrete security, audit, per-agent TTS, workflow-enforcement, and shellward workaround context.
