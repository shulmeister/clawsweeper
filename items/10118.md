## Summary

Keep open. Current main appears to have the core Shift+Enter newline behavior through the pinned pi-tui editor, while Enter still submits, but the issue also asked for documented/help-visible keybinds and optional/configurable behavior. Those parts are still missing in current main, and the later discussion specifically asks for configurability and Ctrl+Enter/IME-friendly behavior.
