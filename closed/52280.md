## Summary

Current main already implements the reported behavior well enough: authenticated loopback probes keep device identity attached so local detail RPCs succeed, and scope-limited read probes are surfaced as degraded reachability instead of unreachable. The user-facing status/probe behavior is documented and shipped in v2026.4.20.
