## Summary

Current main keeps the intentional accountId contract, documents it, and surfaces the confusing multi-account shape through doctor warnings. The reported Feishu behavior is no longer silent from a config/doctor/docs perspective, and the existing wildcard workaround remains the documented way to match every account.
