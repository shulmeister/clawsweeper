## Summary

Current main implements the core request: generated outbound completion messages are write-ahead queued before delivery and recovered after Gateway startup, and restart-triggered notices/continuations are persisted and replayed through the restart sentinel and session-delivery queue. This shipped in v2026.4.24 for restart continuations, with related durable outbound delivery already present on main.

## Evidence

This covers completed replies that were generated before a restart but not yet delivered to the user.
