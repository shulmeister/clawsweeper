## Summary

Current `main` now treats remote CDP profiles as persistent Playwright-backed sessions, reuses the CDP connection across commands, and routes snapshot/tab operations through that session, which addresses the reported Browserbase session-loss behavior.

## Evidence

- **Remote profiles opt into persistent Playwright:** Remote non-loopback CDP profiles are classified with `usesPersistentPlaywright: true`, which is the feature gate for the persistent session path.
