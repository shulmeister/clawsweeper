## Summary

Issue #64912 is fixed on current `main`. Packaged installs now treat the repo-only QA scenario pack as optional, `openclaw completion --write-state` skips subcommand registration failures instead of aborting, and a regression test explicitly covers the reported `qa scenario pack not found: qa/scenarios/index.md` failure mode. The changelog says this shipped in 2026.4.11.

## Evidence
