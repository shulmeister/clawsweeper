## Summary

The reported ordering bug is in the external `@tencent-weixin/openclaw-weixin` channel plugin, not in OpenClaw core. Current main documents WeChat as an external plugin, keeps WeChat runtime ownership outside the core repo, and exposes the generic channel/reply SDK surfaces needed for channel-owned inbound filtering and dispatch.
