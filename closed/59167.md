## Summary

Keep #59167 open. Current main still rejects `trusted-proxy` auth when the proxy source is loopback, even when loopback is explicitly listed in `gateway.trustedProxies` and identity headers are present. The docs also still describe same-host loopback trusted-proxy auth as unsupported. The open PR #59190 and RFC #69066 are relevant follow-up paths, but neither is merged/shipped, so the reported regression is not resolved or safely superseded yet.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and use it as the concrete regression tracker until maintainers either land a focused fix such as #59190, adopt a broader RFC #69066 design, or intentionally document the current loopback rejection as the final supported product boundary with a working same-host alternative.
