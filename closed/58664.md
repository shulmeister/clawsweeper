## Summary

Current `main` already implements Feishu mention-target matching by comparing inbound mention `open_id`s to the current bot's `botOpenId`, blocks `requireMention` replies when the bot was not specifically mentioned, and has regression coverage for non-targeted mentions and broadcast dispatch. The same behavior is present in the shipped `v2026.4.23` release.

## Evidence
