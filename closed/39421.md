## Summary

Current main already handles Slack channel app mentions through the Slack channel plugin, including the same-`ts` message/app_mention race that can make mention-gated channel messages appear ignored. The changelog records the relevant Slack app_mention race fixes as shipped, and regression tests cover dropped and in-flight message/app_mention ordering.
