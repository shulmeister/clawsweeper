## Summary

Current `main` already implements WhatsApp outbound quoted replies: the channel exposes `channels.whatsapp.replyToMode`, resolves `replyToId` into a WhatsApp quote key, converts that into Baileys `quoted` send options, and has tests covering quoted text and media replies.
