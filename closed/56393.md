## Summary

Current `main` already separates user-selected session overrides from auto-created fallback/auth overrides, clears the auto-created ones on `/new` and `sessions.reset`, and documents how to clear a session override and inspect provider auth. That matches the reported "session override suspected" failure mode well enough to close as implemented on `main`.
