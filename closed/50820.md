## Summary

Current `main` already fixes the timed-out `/new` session-memory loss by preserving `previousSessionEntry` during stale-session rollovers, forwarding it into reset hooks, and loading archived reset transcripts for session-memory recovery. The PR's handler-side fallback is obsolete and less targeted than the implementation now on `main`.

## Evidence
