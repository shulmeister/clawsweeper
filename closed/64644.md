## Summary

Current `main` already fixes this MiniMax image-tool failure by treating `MiniMax-M2.7` as text-only for chat and routing MiniMax image understanding through `MiniMax-VL-01` and the dedicated MiniMax VLM endpoint instead of the Anthropic-compatible chat endpoint.
