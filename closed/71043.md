## Summary

Current `main` already normalizes assistant/content-array responses into plain text before outbound delivery, which addresses the reported Telegram `[object Object]` prefix path. The fix is present on `main` after the reported version, but I could not tie it to a released tag yet.

## Evidence

  - file: [src/agents/pi-embedded-utils.ts:84](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/bc21f500d4c14d8ecbacb992c39c7ff0954b04f2/src/agents/pi-embedded-utils.ts#L84)
