## Summary

Current `main` already routes channel send-deps access through the public `openclaw/plugin-sdk/outbound-runtime` seam, and the old relative imports into `src/infra/outbound/send-deps.js` are gone from `extensions/`. The same fix is present in the shipped `v2026.4.22` release commit.

## Evidence
