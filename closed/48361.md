## Summary

Current `main` already covers the silent-finalization paths described in #48361: mid-turn provider overload/rate-limit failures are converted into visible error payloads, empty assistant turns are retried, and exhausted empty/silent-error turns now surface an explicit user-visible failure instead of returning nothing.

## Evidence
