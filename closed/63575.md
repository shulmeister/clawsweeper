## Summary

Current `main` already handles this case: `plugins.allow` disables off-allowlist plugins before config validation/import, so they do not become `status === "error"` entries and therefore do not trip `maybeThrowOnPluginLoadError`. The shipped `v2026.4.23` tag also contains that behavior.

## Evidence
