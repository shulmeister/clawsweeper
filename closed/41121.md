## Summary

Close as `cannot_reproduce`. On current `main` (`e94c0bf51542fe518e49ad4e2ee29882891b5603`), the PR's stated root cause is not supported by the code: inbound user activity does not flip `heartbeatsEnabled`; that flag is only changed by the explicit `set-heartbeats` RPC/UI toggle, while busy main-lane cron already falls back to queued heartbeat and records `ok`. The PR also bundles an unrelated `system-prompt` timestamp change that is not on `main`.
