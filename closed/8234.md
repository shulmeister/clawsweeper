## Summary

Intel AMX acceleration is backend/hardware-specific local inference tuning. OpenClaw already exposes local model integration through bundled provider plugins and custom OpenAI-compatible endpoints, so an AMX-specific setup guide/provider belongs in ClawHub rather than core.
