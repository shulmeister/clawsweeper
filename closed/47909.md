## Summary

Current `main` already auto-splits outbound text at channel-aware limits in the shared delivery path, and Telegram/Discord both wire their channel limits into that path or their own chunked send helpers. The behavior is documented and covered by tests, and the same implementation is present in release `v2026.4.22` (`00bd2cf7a376f1fba26291c6c4766f1f15cbdfa5`).
