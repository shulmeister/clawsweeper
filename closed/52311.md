## Summary

Current main already corrects stale provider/model display mismatches in the Control UI by resolving reported session models against the model catalog and preserving already-qualified refs instead of re-prefixing them with a stale provider. That covers the reported symptom of an OpenAI model being shown under the wrong provider label.

## Evidence
