## Summary

Current main already aborts hung LLM streams and feeds timeout failures into the model fallback chain. The shipped surface is `agents.defaults.llm.idleTimeoutSeconds` rather than the proposed per-model `requestTimeoutMs`, but the requested hang->abort->fallback behavior is implemented and present in release v2026.4.23.

## Evidence
