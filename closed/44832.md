## Summary

Current `main` already addresses the underlying tool-use/tool-result mismatch loop by preventing malformed synthetic tool results, repairing transcript pairing before replay, and re-repairing after truncation/compaction. The PR's narrower error-text handling is obsolete against the current implementation.

## Evidence
