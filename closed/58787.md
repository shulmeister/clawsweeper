## Summary

Current `main` already implements exact Feishu @target gating: Feishu group replies only pass `requireMention` when the current bot's own `open_id` is mentioned, non-target mentions are ignored, post-format `<at user_id>` mentions are parsed, and shared-group targeting is documented through per-agent `groupChat.mentionPatterns`. The requested behavior is therefore already present on `main`, even though the Feishu channel doc only describes `requireMention` at a high level.
