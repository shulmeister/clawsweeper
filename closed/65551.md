## Summary

Keep open. Current main covers important pieces of the report, including cron payload timeouts, per-job fallbacks, and Ollama thinking controls, but the central RAM-pressure termination diagnostics and RAM-aware local model loading behavior remain unimplemented. The related issues cited in #65551 are closed and cover only parts of this issue, not the remaining Apple Silicon MLX/LM Studio/Ollama memory-pressure failure mode.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #65551 open, but narrow it around the remaining core/local-provider reliability gap: better diagnostics for local LM Studio/Ollama termination under macOS memory pressure and a conservative RAM-aware preload/loading warning or refusal path where the provider APIs allow it. The already-covered timeout, fallback, and thinking-control pieces can be treated as shipped context from the related closed issues, but they do not close the RAM-pressure termination problem.
