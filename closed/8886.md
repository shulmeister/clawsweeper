## Summary

Current `main` already routes WhatsApp `documentMessage` attachments through the inbound media downloader, persists them with `saveMediaBuffer`, and assigns `mediaPath` from the saved result. The supporting tests for document MIME/filename handling and persisted inbound media are also present on `main` in commit `1e24bee879f21bd12824010050de3dd9d0e1038e`.

## Evidence
