## Summary

Current `main` already supports configurable exec approval behavior through `tools.exec.host/security/ask`, host-local exec approvals state, and the shipped `openclaw exec-policy` CLI with presets. The request's core goal is implemented, even though the names differ from the proposed `strict/moderate/permissive` labels.
