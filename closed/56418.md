## Summary

Current main already tracks recently sent WhatsApp outbound message IDs and drops matching `fromMe` echoes before they enter the inbound pipeline, including explicit self-chat DM regression coverage. The changelog records this fix in version `2026.3.28`, which is newer than the reported `2026.3.24` build.
