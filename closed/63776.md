## Summary

Current `main` already contains a targeted fix for `sessions_spawn` failing with loopback `close(1008) "pairing required"` during subagent gateway scope upgrades, and it also includes Feishu-specific subagent binding/delivery support with regression coverage. The reported behavior is obsolete against current `main`.
